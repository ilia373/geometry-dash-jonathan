# Sound Files

Place your audio files here:

1. **jump.mp3** - Sound effect for jumping
2. **background.mp3** - Background music (e.g., Astronomia)

## Getting the music

For the Astronomia background music, you can:
1. Download an MP3 version from a legal source
2. Use a YouTube to MP3 converter (for personal use)
3. Use any royalty-free alternative

## Notes

- Supported formats: MP3, WAV, OGG
- Background music will loop automatically
- Jump sound should be short (< 1 second)
